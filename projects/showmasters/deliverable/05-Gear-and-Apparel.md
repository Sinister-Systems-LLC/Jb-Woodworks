# SMPL Gear, Accessories & Crew Apparel

**Compiled:** 2026-05-23

A starter catalog of branded gear and apparel concepts for Show Masters Production Logistics crew. Designed to (1) make the crew look unmistakably SMPL on every dock, (2) give producers a visual identity to remember, and (3) double as recruitment marketing — the gear photos that go on Instagram and TikTok become the most-shared content on social.

Specs included for direct hand-off to a print/embroidery vendor.

---

## Tier 1 — Work apparel (the every-shift uniform)

### 1. SMPL Crew Work Shirt — Dark

The default shift shirt. Worn under high-vis or alone in non-vis venues.

- **Body:** Soft cotton/poly blend, athletic cut, dark charcoal (#1A1A22) or true black
- **Logo placement:** Left chest, embroidered SMPL monogram in gold thread (Pantone 871C / #D4A24A)
- **Back:** Larger "SHOW MASTERS / PRODUCTION LOGISTICS" wordmark in gold across upper back
- **Sizes:** S-3XL, women's cut available
- **Vendor target:** Bella+Canvas 3001 or Next Level 3600 blank, local embroidery shop
- **Estimated cost:** $14-22 per shirt at 100-unit order

### 2. SMPL High-Vis Safety Shirt

For venues requiring ANSI Class 2 visibility.

- **Body:** ANSI Class 2 hi-vis yellow-green
- **Reflective strips:** Horizontal + shoulder
- **Logo:** Left chest BLACK embroidered SMPL monogram (gold doesn't pop on yellow). Back: large "CREW" + small SMPL wordmark
- **Vendor target:** Portwest E170 or Carhartt 100495
- **Estimated cost:** $32-48 per shirt

### 3. SMPL Crew Hoodie — Cold-Weather

For overnight load-outs, winter calls, and the morning coffee shot on Instagram.

- **Body:** Heavyweight (8.5+ oz) pullover, deep charcoal
- **Logo:** Left chest gold-embroidered SMPL monogram
- **Hood interior:** Gold inner lining (the small flex)
- **Back:** Optional large "SHOW MASTERS" wordmark in matte gold vinyl
- **Sleeves:** Cuff stripe in gold
- **Vendor target:** Independent Trading SS4500 or Carhartt K288
- **Estimated cost:** $42-58 per hoodie

### 4. Cargo Work Pants

Practical, durable, branded subtly.

- **Body:** Dickies-style cargo, charcoal or black
- **Logo:** Small gold-embroidered SMPL on right thigh cargo pocket flap
- **Vendor target:** Dickies LP337 base + embroidery
- **Estimated cost:** $42-55 per pair

## Photo / marketing strategy

Once the gear is in hand, run one full photoshoot of the crew geared up:

- One Crew Lead at the dock at 5am, full gear, coffee thermos, clipboard
- A row of 5-6 crew lined up pre-show in matched apparel
- Close-up on the hard hat with the brand mark
- Close-up on the tool roll laid out on a road case
- Crew Lead pinning their enamel pin to a hoodie

These become the recruitment + brand-identity collateral for the next 12 months across Instagram, LinkedIn, the careers page, and trade-publication pitches.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't over-design.** The brand identity is restrained: dark + gold, serif italic accents, monospace mono labels. Apparel that adds drop shadows, gradients, or chromed effects will look like generic vendor merch. Keep it clean.
- **Don't go cheap on hi-vis.** Crew lives in it. Buy the OSHA-rated stuff from real vendors, not the $8 generic ones.
- **Don't print on Yeti / Stanley drinkware.** Always laser-etch. Print scuffs off in 30 days.
- **Don't add the leadership-team names to swag.** Crew turns over; names date the merch.
- **Don't run apparel as a profit center.** This is uniform + marketing, not retail.
