# 03 ,  Google Business Profile (GMB) Ranking Guide

The Google Maps "3-pack" that shows up when someone searches `stagehand staffing orlando` is the **most valuable real estate on the internet** for a local services business. Three businesses get shown. We need to be one of them in Orlando AND Dallas.

Here's the exact recipe to dominate it.

---

## Step 1 ,  Claim BOTH profiles (Orlando + Dallas)

You need TWO separate Google Business Profiles, one per office:

- **Orlando, FL** ,  4906 Patch Road, Orlando, FL 32822
- **Dallas, TX** ,  Dallas, TX 75201

Go to https://business.google.com → Add business → search for each one. If the listing already exists (likely), click "Claim this business" and verify. Verification is usually by postcard (5-7 days) or instant phone/email if you've claimed before.

**Important:** Each profile is its own ranking. Each gets its own reviews, posts, photos, and metrics. You can't shortcut by using one profile for both.

---

## Step 2 ,  Fill EVERY field to 100%

Google ranks profiles partly by completeness. Half-filled profiles never crack the 3-pack.

### Required (do these for both profiles)

| Field | What to put |
|---|---|
| Business name | `Show Masters Production Logistics` (do NOT add keywords like ",  Stagehand Staffing Orlando" ,  that's a guideline violation that gets profiles suspended) |
| Address | Exact street address (same as the site &amp; sitemap) |
| Service area | List 25-30 cities you serve from that office |
| Phone | `(877) 765-2267` for both, OR set up local DIDs (recommended): `(407) XXX-XXXX` for Orlando + `(214) XXX-XXXX` for Dallas. Local numbers help local rank. |
| Website | `https://www.showmasters.com/orlando` for the Orlando profile, `/dallas` for the Dallas profile |
| Hours | Real business hours. If you take overnight calls, set "Open 24 hours" with a note. |
| Category ,  primary | `Event Production Company` (this is the single most important ranking signal) |
| Category ,  secondary (up to 9) | `Staffing Agency`, `Audio Visual Equipment Supplier`, `Rigging Equipment Supplier`, `Talent Agency`, `Convention Center`, `Stage Lighting Equipment Supplier`, `Production Company`, `Trade Fair Organizer`, `Equipment Rental Agency` |
| From the business | Short tagline: "Live event stagehands, riggers, and technicians for productions across Florida and the Southeast since 2002." |

### Description (750 characters max)

Write a different one for each profile. Example for Orlando:

> Show Masters Production Logistics has been providing experienced stagehands, riggers, technicians, lift operators, and crew leads to live event producers in Orlando and across Florida since 2002. From our Orlando, FL headquarters we staff corporate events, concerts, festivals, trade shows, conventions, theater productions, sporting events, houses of worship, and private events. Every staffer is a W-4 employee covered by our worker's comp + general liability insurance. Detailed invoices within 72 hours of load-out. No hidden fees, no admin percentages. Locations served include Orlando, Kissimmee, Lake Mary, Winter Park, Tampa, Daytona Beach, and surrounding markets.

Use the same template for Dallas, swap the cities.

### Services (every service = its own GMB Service entry)

Add each of these as a separate service with a short description and price-on-request:

- Stagehand staffing
- Rigging crew
- Audio technicians
- Lighting technicians
- Video technicians
- Camera operators
- Spotlight operators
- Lift and forklift operators
- Crew leads &amp; show management
- Trade show I&amp;D labor
- Labor logistics consulting

## Step 3 ,  Photos (the biggest local-pack lever after reviews)

Profiles with 100+ photos get **520% more requests** than profiles with under 10. Upload **at least 25 photos per profile to start**, add 2-4 more every week.

### What to upload

- **Logo** ,  use `pfp-square.svg` exported to PNG at 250×250 minimum.
- **Cover photo** ,  use `og-card.svg` exported to PNG at 1024×576.
- **Interior** ,  photos of the office.
- **Exterior** ,  the building from the street.
- **At work** ,  crew on a live site (load-in, rigging, audio console, lighting truss). 10-20 of these. These are the highest-converting photos.
- **Team** ,  staff portraits + crew group shots.
- **Equipment** ,  trucks, lifts, cases, ready to roll.
- **Behind-the-scenes** ,  load-in chaos shots, sunrise on the dock, the calm before doors.

### Photo SEO

For every uploaded photo, before upload:
- Rename the file: `show-masters-stagehand-orlando-corporate-event-2026.jpg` (kebab-case, includes city + service)
- Add geo-EXIF if you can ,  tools like [GeoImgr](https://www.geoimgr.com/) tag a photo with the Orlando office coordinates
- Upload at full resolution (Google compresses; don't pre-compress)

## Step 4 ,  Reviews (this is the #1 ranking lever)

The Orlando profile needs to **out-review every competitor** in Orlando. Same for Dallas.

Target: **100+ reviews on each profile within 12 months. 4.8/5 average.**

How:

1. After every successful event, the project lead asks the client: *"We loved working with you. Would you take 60 seconds to leave us a Google review? Here's the link."*
2. Send a short link via text or email: use [Whitespark's review link tool](https://whitespark.ca/google-review-link-generator/) or just grab it from the GBP dashboard.
3. **Respond to EVERY review** within 24 hours, including 5-star ones. Use the client's name. Reference the event ("Thanks for the kind words on the [Event Name] load-in!").
4. For 1- and 2-star reviews: respond professionally, take it offline, document what happened, never argue publicly.

### Review request script (text message)

> Hey [Name] ,  great working with you on the [Event] this weekend. If you have 60 seconds, could you leave us a quick Google review? [LINK] Means everything to our crew. ,  SMPL

That's it. ~60% conversion when sent within 24 hours of the show ending.

### Don't do these (will get the profile suspended)

- Don't pay for reviews or trade them
- Don't review your own profile from any account
- Don't ask clients to review while they're physically inside your office (geofencing flag)
- Don't gate reviews ("only leave one if it's 5 stars") ,  this is illegal under FTC guidelines

## Step 5 ,  Google Posts (weekly, forever)

Every Monday morning, post something on both profiles. It takes 5 minutes and Google rewards active profiles.

Post types that work:

- **What's new** ,  "We crewed a 600-person corporate plenary at Orange County Convention Center this week."
- **Offer** ,  "Free labor estimate for any FL event load-in confirmed by [date]."
- **Event** ,  "We'll be at LDI in Las Vegas Nov 12-14 ,  come find us at booth #X."
- **Product** ,  Highlight a service (e.g. "I&amp;D labor for trade shows in Orlando + Dallas").

Add a photo to every post. Add a CTA button. Posts roll off after 7 days, so consistency matters more than perfection.

---

## Step 6 ,  Q&amp;A section (own the narrative)

In the Q&amp;A section, **post questions yourself** (from a personal account) and then answer them as the business. Examples:

- Q: "Do you provide stagehands for corporate events in Orlando?"
- A: "Yes ,  we've staffed corporate events across Orlando, Kissimmee, and Lake Mary since 2002. Most common roles: audio, lighting, video stagehands, plus a Crew Lead per shift. Call (877) 765-2267 for an estimate."

This is GoogleSEO-approved (it's an FAQ tool) and lets you stuff target keywords into your profile naturally.

Add 10 Q&amp;As per profile to start.

## Step 7 ,  UTM-tagged website links

When you set the GMB website URL, use UTM tags so Google Analytics shows you exactly how much traffic / leads come from GBP:

`https://www.showmasters.com/orlando?utm_source=gmb&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=orlando-gbp`

Same for Dallas. Now you can prove ROI per profile.

---

## Step 8 ,  Track rank weekly with Local Falcon

[Local Falcon](https://www.localfalcon.com/) ($24/mo) shows a heatmap of your GBP ranking across a city ,  "you're #1 at the office, #4 three miles east, #12 at the airport." It tells you exactly which neighborhoods you need to push more in.

Run a scan once a week on each profile. Track the score over time. Adjust posting + photo strategy based on which neighborhoods are weak.

---

## Step 9 ,  Citations (NAP consistency)

Every directory that lists you must have the EXACT same Name, Address, Phone. Even tiny mismatches (`Blvd` vs `Boulevard`) hurt ranking.

See `08-LOCAL-CITATIONS.md` for the full list.

## Step 10 ,  Messages, bookings, and the call attribution

Turn on:

- **Messaging** ,  producers can text you straight from the profile. Respond within 5 minutes during business hours.
- **Booking link** ,  point it at `contact.html#estimate`.
- **Call attribution** ,  tracks how many calls came from the profile (free, just turn it on).
