# 01 ,  Marketing Plan (12-Month, Plain English)

**Goal:** Get Show Masters booked on more shows, especially in Florida and Texas, by becoming the obvious first-call for any producer who needs live event crew in either state.

We have three things working in our favor:

1. **22+ years in business** (since 2002) ,  nobody buying labor wants a 2-year-old company.
2. **1,700+ clients served** ,  the social proof is already there.
3. **Two strategic hubs** (Orlando + Dallas) and 33-state experience.

Most competitors are local-only. SMPL's *combination* of "deep local roots in FL + TX" + "experienced traveling crew" is the wedge.

---

## The 4 marketing pillars

We're going to invest in four channels, in this order of importance:

### Pillar 1 ,  Local SEO + Google Business Profile (months 1-12, ongoing)
**Why first:** When a producer in Orlando types `"stagehand staffing orlando"`, we should appear in the top 3 of the Google Maps "local pack" AND the #1 organic result. That's free, recurring lead flow that never stops.

Read: `02-SEO-STRATEGY.md`, `03-GMB-RANKING.md`, `08-LOCAL-CITATIONS.md`.

**Expected impact:** 8-15 inbound leads per month per market once the local pack is locked in (~ month 4-6).

### Pillar 2 ,  Direct outbound to production companies (months 1-6, hire-driven)
**Why:** Most event labor in FL + TX is booked by ~300 production companies. We can name them. Cold-email + cold-call each one with a tight pitch. Even a 2% conversion = 6 new accounts.

Read: `07-OUTREACH-LIST.md`.

**Expected impact:** 4-8 new production-company accounts in the first 90 days.

### Pillar 3 ,  Content + thought-leadership (months 2-12)
**Why:** Producers Google "how to budget stagehand labor" and "rigging cost per hour." When SMPL is the answer, we're the trusted authority by the time they request an estimate.

Read: `06-CONTENT-CALENDAR.md`.

**Expected impact:** Triples our organic traffic by month 8; adds Google Site Links + featured snippets.

### Pillar 4 ,  Reviews + referrals flywheel (months 1-12)
**Why:** Every new 5-star Google review = local pack ranking boost AND social proof. Every referral from a happy client costs us $0 to acquire.

Read: `09-REVIEWS.md`.

**Expected impact:** 50+ Google reviews in 12 months (most competitors have &lt;15). Referrals become 20-30% of bookings.

## Quarterly plan

### Q1 ,  "Foundation"
- Claim + optimize both Google Business Profiles to 100%.
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console.
- Build out the on-page SEO (already in this site).
- Get listed on the 30 directories in `08-LOCAL-CITATIONS.md`.
- Launch 2 location landing pages (Orlando, Dallas).
- Ask 30 existing clients for Google reviews.
- Send 200 cold emails to FL + TX producers.

**KPI:** GMB calls + direction requests start trending up. 15+ new Google reviews. 2 closed cold-outbound wins.

### Q2 ,  "Compound"
- Publish 4 long-form blog posts (one a month) per `06-CONTENT-CALENDAR.md`.
- Launch city-specific landing pages for Tampa, Miami, Dallas, Houston, Austin.
- Begin GMB weekly Posts (offers, news, events).
- Reach 30 Google reviews on the main profile.
- Send 200 more cold emails.

**KPI:** Top-3 local pack ranking in Orlando for at least 3 buyer keywords. 5+ direct booking inquiries per month from the site.

### Q3 ,  "Authority"
- Begin pitching to industry trade publications (Live Design Online, Event Marketer, BizBash).
- Launch a quarterly "State of Event Labor in Florida / Texas" report (PDF download with email capture).
- Start a YouTube channel: behind-the-scenes load-in footage + crew profiles.
- Add a Case Studies section to the site (3 detailed wins).

**KPI:** 3+ press mentions or guest posts. 75+ Google reviews. 30%+ MoM organic traffic growth.

### Q4 ,  "Lock-in"
- Renew + refresh the cold-outreach list (research who switched producers).
- Launch a referral program: $250 thank-you for any client introduction that turns into a booked show.
- Begin paid Google Search ads on the 20 highest-intent keywords in FL + TX (small budget, $500/mo per market to start).
- Year-end content: "Top 100 events SMPL crewed in 2026."

**KPI:** Double Q1 inbound leads. 100+ Google reviews. Predictable lead pipeline.

## The non-negotiables

These are the things you must do consistently or the whole plan stalls:

1. **Answer every Google review within 24 hours.** (Yes, even the 5-stars. Especially the 1-stars.)
2. **Post on Google Business Profile every Monday.** Even a photo + a sentence counts.
3. **Send a follow-up email 3 days after every estimate.** Half of all wins come from follow-up.
4. **Add one new city-specific page or blog post per month.** Compound interest on Google.
5. **Ask for a review at the end of every successful show.** "We loved working with you ,  would you take 60 seconds to leave us a Google review?" Two-thirds will say yes.

That's it. Boring, repeated, every week.
