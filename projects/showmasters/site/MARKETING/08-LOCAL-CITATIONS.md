# 08 — Local Citations: The Directory List

A "citation" is any web page that lists our business Name, Address, and Phone (NAP). Google uses citation consistency as a ranking signal for the local pack. Every directory we're on with consistent NAP = one more vote toward "this business is real and locally rooted."

Target: 35 citations within 90 days. 50 within 12 months. NAP-consistent everywhere.

---

## The non-negotiable: NAP consistency

Pick the EXACT formatting for each office and use it identically everywhere. Tiny variations break the citation signal.

### Orlando office (canonical NAP)

```
Show Masters Production Logistics
4906 Patch Road
Orlando, FL 32822
(877) 765-2267
Orders@ShowMasters.com
```

### Dallas office (canonical NAP)

```
Show Masters Production Logistics
[Dallas office address — to confirm]
Dallas, TX 75201
(877) 765-2267
Orders@ShowMasters.com
```

Notes:
- Always "Show Masters Production Logistics" (4 words, title case). Never SMPL, never "ShowMasters."
- Always "Boulevard" (not "Blvd."). Always "Road" (not "Rd.").
- Always "Orders@ShowMasters.com" (capitalization preserved — even though email is case-insensitive, consistency helps citation matching).

## Citation hygiene rules

1. **One person owns the spreadsheet** of all citations. Single source of truth.
2. **Every login gets stored in a password manager** (1Password, Bitwarden — never a plaintext sheet).
3. **Quarterly NAP audit:** Whitespark's free Local Citation Finder shows where NAP varies. Fix every drift.
4. **Update on change:** If we add a third location or change the office phone, every citation gets updated in 30 days.
5. **Photos on every directory that allows them.** Logo + 3–5 work shots minimum. Improves CTR everywhere.

## Tools

- **Whitespark Local Citation Finder** — finds existing citations + suggests new ones. $20/mo when actively building, $0 when in maintenance.
- **Moz Local** — pushes updates to dozens of directories at once. $14/mo. Use after the initial 35 are hand-claimed.
- **BrightLocal Citation Tracker** — alerts when a directory changes our listing. $29/mo.

Hand-claim the first 35 (Tier 1 + 2). Then automate the long tail with Moz Local.

---

## What we DO NOT do

- **No paid citation packs from random "SEO services."** They scrape low-quality directories and submit garbage links. Hurts more than helps.
- **No fake addresses.** Some companies invent virtual offices in cities they don't serve. Google catches this and penalizes the whole profile.
- **No keyword stuffing the business name.** "Show Masters Production Logistics — Orlando Stagehand Staffing Inc." breaks Google's guidelines. The name field is for the actual business name. Period.

---

## How to measure if citations are working

Three months after the Tier 1 + 2 push:

- Both GMB profiles should be ranking in the local 3-pack for at least 2 of their target keywords.
- Direct site visits attributed to "Other" sources in Analytics should be up 30%+ (this is citation referrals).
- GMB calls + direction requests should be up month-over-month for at least 4 consecutive months.

If those numbers aren't moving, the issue isn't citations — it's reviews or photos. Loop back to `03-GMB-RANKING.md` Step 3 and Step 4.
